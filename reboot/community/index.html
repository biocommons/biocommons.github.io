    Mailing Lists

    • biocommons-announce Low-volume, read-only announcements of new releases and other information.

    • biocommons-devs List for developers of biocommons tools.

    • biocommons-users List for users to ask (and answer!) questions about tools.

    • hgvs-discuss List to discuss hgvs (the package) and HGVS (the recommendations)

    • biocommons-devops High-volume list with automated messages from ci/cd bots.
